Blog Post #2 | My Passion

If you know me in real life, you probably know that I love languages. I love everything about them and even just having the ability to speak some words means a lot to me. I've been learning for exactly two years in a row as of today (05/01/24) languages on Duolingo. I now know German & French very well while now I'm learning russian. I think this would be a great opportunity for me to try a new type of language. For me, I've always wanted to try learning ASL (or sign-language). I've started a few times before but never got to a point where I knew it well, I couldn't even introduce myself. For this challenge, I want to learn ASL and be on the level of introducing myself including talking about my name (even just in an initial), where I'm from, and things I like to do. In my opinion, even being able to speak at this level is still a valuable skill. This also opens up other opportunities for me in the future. Say I really like ASL, then I'll already understand some basics and it'll be easy for me to continue!
