Blog Post #3 Update

So recently, I’ve been working on memorising my ASL. I actually thought at the start of this activity that remembering hand signs would be really hard and that verbal languages would be much easier. Now, I might feel different. After beginning with my name and the spelling[1], I tried to practice it over and over until I felt I could do it well and most importantly, quickly. The key part would be doing this fast because I find it really helps with memorisation. After being able to do my name well, I went on to things like age[2] which were pretty easy due to the fact that it was only one extra sign. Spelling where I live[3] was simple due to me already knowing the alphabet pretty well which really speeded up my learning. Finally, I learned how to do things I like[4] such as coding[5] or going outside[6] which was a little difficult but it turned out okay in the end. After all this learning, I’m convinced that I could introduce myself well and talk a little bit to someone deaf due to some similarity of signs to actions.
